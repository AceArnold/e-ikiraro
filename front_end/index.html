About E-ikiraro

E-Ikiraro is a simple online platform that helps Burundians access key government services quickly and conveniently.

E-Ikiraro is a digital platform designed to make access to public services in Burundi fast, simple, and transparent. It allows citizens to apply for key documents such as national IDs, passports, and driver’s licenses online — reducing long queues, travel costs, and manual paperwork. The system is built to match Burundi’s digital needs, ensuring ease of use, mobile access, and secure data handling. E-Ikiraro aims to bridge the gap between citizens and government services by providing a modern, reliable, and user-friendly solution.
